I am a second-year M.S. student at the [School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering](https://dz.sdut.edu.cn/), [Shandong University of Technology (SDUT)](https://www.sdut.edu.cn/). Advised by [Prof. Mingliang Gao (È´òÊòé‰∫Æ)](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=IFEIrUgAAAAJ&hl=en), my research is centered on the critical challenges of misinformation and malicious content detection.
